## About Franklee Group

A South African logistics and supply chain group spanning:
1. **Truck Wash** — Fleet washing and compliance hygiene
2. **Logistics & Warehousing** — National distribution and storage
3. **Supply Chain Consultancy** — Network design and route optimisation
4. **Franklee Loop** — Backhaul exchange: filling empty return legs with paying loads

## About Franklee Loop

A digital marketplace matching Shippers (loads to move) with verified Drivers via the Franklee Admin platform. The deal is struck between Shipper and Admin, and Drivers execute on instruction.

**User roles:**
- Driver — registers, marks pre-attendance, receives assignments, executes delivery
- Shipper — posts haul requests, negotiates rate with Admin, tracks load
- Admin (Franklee) — verifies drivers and vehicles, reviews load requests, negotiates price, assigns drivers
