HOW IT WORKS

  1. 01

    Post Your Return Leg

    Carriers with empty return capacity log their route, load specs, and availability window. No upfront cost — you only pay when matched.

  2. 02

    Shippers Request Capacity

    Businesses needing flexible freight capacity browse available return-leg trucks on their required corridor and request a match.

  3. 03

    Franklee Confirms the Match

    Our team confirms compatibility, rate, and timing. We handle the coordination so both parties can focus on operations — not admin.

  4. 04

    Revenue Recovered, Loop Closed

    Carrier earns on a run that was previously dead cost. Shipper saves on spot rates. Franklee charges 10% on the matched load value — success-only.
